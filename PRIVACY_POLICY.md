# Privacy Policy for MyTaskList
**Last updated:** April 18, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **MyTaskList - Simple To Do List**. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how our application handles your data. Our app is designed to help you manage your tasks and priorities locally on your device.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage
Our application is built with a "Privacy-First" approach. 

* **Local Storage:** All task lists, priorities, and completion statuses you enter are stored strictly on your device's internal memory. We do not have access to your tasks.
* **No Cloud Sync:** Your data never leaves your phone and is not uploaded to any external servers or cloud services.
* **No Personal Account:** You can use all features of the app without creating an account or providing an email address.

## 3. Advertising and Third-Party Services
This app uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. To serve ads, Google may collect and use certain data, such as:

* **Device Identifiers:** Such as the Advertising ID.
* **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with ads within the app.

We use this data only to support the continued development of the application. For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit: [https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## 4. Children’s Privacy
Our services are not designed for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take necessary actions.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion
* **Retention:** Your task list is retained on your device locally to ensure your data is available whenever you open the app.
* **Manual Deletion:** You have full control over your data. You can delete individual tasks or clear your entire list within the app settings.
* **Uninstallation:** If you uninstall the app or clear the app's cache/data in your device settings, all your stored tasks will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

## 6. Data Security
We value your trust. Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using device-level security (such as a screen lock or biometric authentication) to protect your data.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
